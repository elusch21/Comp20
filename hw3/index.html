Ultimate Scholarships Homepage

Passionate about Ultimate and attending University? You've come to the right place.

Ultimate Scholarships is a national nonprofit organization dedicated to providing college scholarships for those pursing the great sport of Ultimate Frisbee. Founded in 2020 by our visionary CEO, Ultimate Scholarships partners with colleges and USAU to provide oppportunities for students looking to pursue their Ultimate passion in college.
